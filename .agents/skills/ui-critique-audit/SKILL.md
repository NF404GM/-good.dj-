1. [AGENTS.md](C:\Users\tahme\OneDrive\Desktop\good.dj\AGENTS.md)

Review every surface against this checklist:

- Is the hierarchy obvious at a glance?
- Does the performance surface dominate the shell correctly?
- Are the controls readable at performance distance?
- Are empty, loading, ready, active, synced, and error states honest?
- Is spacing rhythmic, or are panels fighting each other?
- Is color being used for meaning, not decoration?
- Does motion clarify state rather than distract?
- Does the surface feel like good. software, not borrowed competition styling?

When asked for a review, prioritize findings over compliments.
Order findings by severity and include file references.

If screenshots are provided, review the image and the implementation together.
